What are These?

These are what people refer to as the zodiac signs. They originate as constellations which are groups of stars that form a identifiable shape which people can recognize. The 12 signs correspond to the time of year which is why people born during certain dates are a part of that zodiac sign group. For example someone born on October 19th would be a part of the Libra zodiac group and thus considered a Libra. Likewise every other day has a corresponding zodiac group. Each zodiac sign group has certain dates as to when people can be born into that certain zodiac sign group and usually is about a 30 day period. Each zodiac group comes with different descriptions on how those people born into the groups act and behave. For the most part everyone that is born into a certain zodiac sign group will behave as intended according to their sign, but their are cases where a person can attain different personality traits and act as a different sign or multiple different signs. When a person does not follow their zodiac signs behavior traits they are refered to as TFBP or two-faced-birthday-people because they are believing that they have they traits of other zodiac groups when they should not.
